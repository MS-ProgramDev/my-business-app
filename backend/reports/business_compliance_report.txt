### Gas System Approval

**Name**
Gas System Approval

**What it is and why it's needed**
Approval for the safe installation and operation of gas systems in businesses.

**Steps to comply**
1. Ensure that all gas systems are installed and maintained by a licensed professional.
2. Submit a request for gas system approval to the relevant authority.
3. Provide documentation and inspection results as required.
4. Address any deficiencies or concerns raised by the authority.
5. Obtain written approval from the authority.

**Estimated cost (in NIS)**
500 - 2000

**Estimated time to complete**
2–6 weeks

**Responsible authority**
Ministry of Energy and the Israel Gas Authority

**Priority level**
High

**Useful links**
https://www.gov.il/he/departments/israel_gas_authority – Use this site to find information on gas system regulations and approval processes.
https://www.gov.il/he/departments/minstry_of_energy – Use this site to find information on energy and gas regulations.


### Meat Handling License

**Name**
Meat Handling License

**What it is and why it's needed**
License required for businesses that handle, store, or serve meat products to ensure food safety.

**Steps to comply**
1. Ensure that all staff handling meat products are trained in food safety and handling practices.
2. Implement proper meat storage and handling procedures.
3. Obtain a meat handling license from the Israel Ministry of Health.
4. Pass regular inspections to maintain the license.
5. Display the license in a visible location.

**Estimated cost (in NIS)**
1000 - 5000

**Estimated time to complete**
4–12 weeks

**Responsible authority**
Israel Ministry of Health

**Priority level**
High

**Useful links**
https://www.gov.il/he/departments/moh – Use this site to find information on food safety regulations and licensing.
https://www.gov.il/he/departments/food_safety – Download this guide to understand meat handling requirements.
