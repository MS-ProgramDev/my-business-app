Here are the compliance reports for each requirement:

**1. Meat Handling License: Required if business serves meat**

* **Name**: Meat Handling License (רישיון לטיפול בבשר)
* **What it is and why it's needed**: A license required for businesses that handle, process, or serve meat products to ensure food safety and prevent the spread of diseases. This license ensures that the business follows proper handling, storage, and preparation procedures for meat products.
* **Steps to comply**:
	1. Submit an application to the Israeli Ministry of Health (משרד הבריאות) with required documentation, including a business license and proof of proper food handling and storage facilities.
	2. Pass an inspection by the Ministry of Health to verify compliance with food safety regulations.
	3. Pay the required license fee.
* **Estimated cost (in NIS)**: 500 - 2,000 (dependent on the type and size of the business)
* **Estimated time to complete**: 2-6 weeks
* **Responsible authority**: Israeli Ministry of Health (משרד הבריאות)
* **Priority level**: High
* **Useful links**: https://www.gov.il/en/topics/health/food-safety

**2. Delivery Registration: For delivery-based businesses**

* **Name**: Delivery Registration (רישום למשלוחים)
* **What it is and why it's needed**: A registration requirement for businesses that offer delivery services to ensure compliance with food safety regulations and consumer protection laws. This registration helps track and monitor food delivery businesses.
* **Steps to comply**:
	1. Register with the Israeli Ministry of Economy and Industry (משרד הכלכלה והתעשייה) as a delivery-based business.
	2. Provide required documentation, including a business license and proof of food handling and storage facilities.
* **Estimated cost (in NIS)**: 0 - 100 (registration fee)
* **Estimated time to complete**: 1-3 days
* **Responsible authority**: Israeli Ministry of Economy and Industry (משרד הכלכלה והתעשייה)
* **Priority level**: Medium
* **Useful links**: https://www.gov.il/en/topics/economy/consumer-protection

**3. Fire Department Approval: Fire safety approval required for large spaces**

* **Name**: Fire Department Approval (אישור משטרת האש)
* **What it is and why it's needed**: A fire safety approval required for businesses that occupy large spaces to ensure compliance with fire safety regulations and prevent fires. This approval ensures that the business has implemented necessary fire safety measures.
* **Steps to comply**:
	1. Submit an application to the Israeli Fire Department (משטרת האש) with required documentation, including a business license and building plans.
	2. Pass an inspection by the Fire Department to verify compliance with fire safety regulations.
	3. Pay the required approval fee.
* **Estimated cost (in NIS)**: 500 - 5,000 (dependent on the size and complexity of the business)
* **Estimated time to complete**: 4-12 weeks
* **Responsible authority**: Israeli Fire Department (משטרת האש)
* **Priority level**: High
* **Useful links**: https://www.gov.il/en/topics/fire-safety

**4. Police Exemption Notice: Exempt from police requirements under certain conditions**

* **Name**: Police Exemption Notice (הודעת פטור ממשטרה)
* **What it is and why it's needed**: A notice that exempts certain businesses from police requirements, such as background checks and security clearances. This notice is required for businesses that handle sensitive information or materials.
* **Steps to comply**:
	1. Determine if the business is eligible for an exemption from police requirements.
	2. Submit an application to the Israeli Police (משטרת ישראל) with required documentation, including a business license and proof of eligibility for exemption.
	3. Pay the required notice fee (if applicable).
* **Estimated cost (in NIS)**: 0 - 100 (notice fee)
* **Estimated time to complete**: 1-4 weeks
* **Responsible authority**: Israeli Police (משטרת ישראל)
* **Priority level**: Medium
* **Useful links**: https://www.gov.il/en/topics/police

**5. General Sanitation Requirements: Every business must adhere to basic hygiene regulations**

* **Name**: General Sanitation Requirements (דרישות סניטציה כלליות)
* **What it is and why it's needed**: Basic hygiene regulations that all businesses must follow to ensure a clean and safe environment for customers and employees. These regulations cover aspects such as waste disposal, cleaning schedules, and employee hygiene.
* **Steps to comply**:
	1. Develop and implement a sanitation plan that meets the requirements outlined in the Official Israeli specification 'מפרט אחיד פריט4.2 א׳ – בית אוכל / מסעדה (כולל משלוח ושתייה לשימוש במקום)' (Section 4.2).
	2. Train employees on sanitation procedures and protocols.
	3. Conduct regular sanitation inspections to ensure compliance.
* **Estimated cost (in NIS)**: 0 - 1,000 (dependent on the size and complexity of the business)
* **Estimated time to complete**: Ongoing
* **Responsible authority**: Israeli Ministry of Health (משרד הבריאות) and local authorities
* **Priority level**: High
* **Useful links**: https://www.gov.il/BlobFolder/generalpage/uniform-spec-04-02a/he/18-07-2022_4.2A.pdf (Section 4.2)
