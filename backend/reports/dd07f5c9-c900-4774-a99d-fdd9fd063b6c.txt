### Gas System Approval

**Name**
Gas System Approval

**What it is and why it's needed**
Approval for the safe installation and operation of gas systems in food businesses to prevent accidents and ensure customer safety.

**Steps to comply**
1. Ensure that all gas systems are installed and maintained by a licensed professional.
2. Obtain approval from a certified inspector or authority.
3. Ensure that all gas equipment meets Israeli standards.
4. Regularly inspect and maintain gas systems.

### Delivery Registration

**Name**
Delivery Registration

**What it is and why it's needed**
Registration for businesses that offer delivery services to ensure compliance with food safety regulations during transportation.

**Steps to comply**
1. Register with the local authority.
2. Ensure that delivery vehicles meet food safety standards.
3. Train delivery personnel on food handling procedures.
4. Implement a system for tracking and monitoring deliveries.

### General Sanitation Requirements

**Name**
General Sanitation Requirements

**What it is and why it's needed**
Basic hygiene regulations that all food businesses must follow to ensure a clean and safe environment for food preparation and consumption.

**Steps to comply**
1. Implement regular cleaning and disinfection schedules.
2. Ensure proper waste disposal.
3. Provide adequate handwashing facilities.
4. Train staff on hygiene practices.

**Estimated cost (in NIS)**
1000 - 5000

**Estimated time to complete**
Ongoing

**Responsible authority**
Ministry of Health

**Priority level**
High

**Useful links**
https://www.health.gov.il/English/ministry/Pages/HealthDivisions.aspx
