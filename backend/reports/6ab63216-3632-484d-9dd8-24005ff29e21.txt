### Gas System Approval

**Name**
Gas System Approval

**What it is and why it's needed**
Approval for the safe installation and operation of gas systems in businesses.

**Steps to comply**
1. Ensure that all gas systems are installed and maintained by a licensed professional.
2. Submit an application for gas system approval to the relevant authorities.
3. Provide documentation and inspection results as required.
4. Address any deficiencies or concerns raised by the authorities.
5. Obtain a certificate of approval.

**Estimated cost (in NIS)**
500 - 2000

**Estimated time to complete**
2–6 weeks

**Responsible authority**
Ministry of Energy and the Israel Gas Authority

**Priority level**
High

**Useful links**
https://www.gov.il/he/departments/israeli_gas_authority – Use this site to find information on gas system regulations and approval processes.
https://www.gov.il/he/departments/minstry_of_energy – Use this site to find information on energy and gas regulations.


### Meat Handling License

**Name**
Meat Handling License

**What it is and why it's needed**
A license required for businesses that handle, store, or serve meat products to ensure food safety.

**Steps to comply**
1. Ensure that your business meets the requirements for meat handling as outlined in the regulations.
2. Submit an application for a meat handling license to the relevant authorities.
