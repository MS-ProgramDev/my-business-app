### Gas System Approval

**Name**
Gas System Approval

**What it is and why it's needed**
Approval for the safe installation and operation of gas systems in businesses.

**Steps to comply**
1. Ensure that all gas installations are performed by a licensed technician.
2. Obtain a certificate of conformity from the installer.
3. Submit the certificate and system details to the relevant authorities for approval.

**Estimated cost (in NIS)**
Not specified

**Estimated time to complete**
Not specified

**Responsible authority**
The Israeli Ministry of Energy and the local authorities.

**Priority level**
High

**Useful links**
https://www.gov.il/he/departments/israeli-ministry-of-energy – Contact the Ministry of Energy for guidance on gas system regulations and approval processes.


### Meat Handling License

**Name**
Meat Handling License

**What it is and why it's needed**
A license required for businesses that handle and serve meat products to ensure food safety.

1. Ensure that all staff handling meat products are trained in food safety.
