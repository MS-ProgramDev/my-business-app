Here are the compliance reports for each requirement:

**Gas System Approval**

* **Name**: Gas System Approval
* **What it is and why it's needed**: This approval is required for businesses that use gas for cooking, heating, or other purposes. The approval ensures that the gas system is installed, maintained, and operated safely to prevent accidents and injuries. According to the Official Israeli specification 'מפרט אחיד פריט4.2 א׳ – בית אוכל / מסעדה (כולל משלוח ושתייה לשימוש במקום)', Section 3.1.2, a gas system approval is required for restaurants and food establishments that use gas.
* **Steps to comply**:
	1. Ensure that the gas system is installed by a licensed technician.
	2. Obtain a certificate of conformity from the installer.
	3. Submit the certificate to the Israeli Gas Authority for approval.
* **Estimated cost (in NIS)**: 500 - 2,000 (depending on the complexity of the system and the installer)
* **Estimated time to complete**: 1-3 weeks
* **Responsible authority**: Israeli Gas Authority
* **Priority level**: High
* **Useful links**: 
	+ https://www.gov.il/en/departments/israeli-gas-authority
	+ https://www.gov.il/BlobFolder/generalpage/uniform-spec-04-02a/he/18-07-2022_4.2A.pdf (Section 3.1.2)

**Police Exemption Notice**

* **Name**: Police Exemption Notice
* **What it is and why it's needed**: This notice is required for businesses that are exempt from certain police requirements, such as installing a security system or having a safe. The exemption is granted under certain conditions, such as the business being located in a secure area or having a low-risk profile. According to the Official Israeli specification 'מפרט אחיד פריט4.2 א׳ – בית אוכל / מסעדה (כולל משלוח ושתייה לשימוש במקום)', Section 5.2, a police exemption notice may be required for restaurants and food establishments.
* **Steps to comply**:
	1. Determine if your business meets the exemption criteria.
	2. Submit an application to the Israel Police for an exemption notice.
	3. Provide required documentation, such as business registration and proof of address.
* **Estimated cost (in NIS)**: 0 - 500 (depending on the complexity of the application)
* **Estimated time to complete**: 1-4 weeks
* **Responsible authority**: Israel Police
* **Priority level**: Medium
* **Useful links**: 
	+ https://www.gov.il/en/departments/israel-police
	+ https://www.gov.il/BlobFolder/generalpage/uniform-spec-04-02a/he/18-07-2022_4.2A.pdf (Section 5.2)

**General Sanitation Requirements**

* **Name**: General Sanitation Requirements
* **What it is and why it's needed**: These requirements ensure that businesses maintain basic hygiene standards to prevent the spread of diseases and maintain a clean environment for customers and employees. According to the Official Israeli specification 'מפרט אחיד פריט4.2 א׳ – בית אוכל / מסעדה (כולל משלוח ושתייה לשימוש במקום)', Section 2.1, general sanitation requirements are applicable to all restaurants and food establishments.
* **Steps to comply**:
	1. Ensure that the business premises are clean and well-maintained.
	2. Implement proper waste disposal and recycling procedures.
	3. Provide adequate handwashing facilities and ensure that employees follow proper hygiene practices.
	4. Regularly inspect the premises to ensure compliance.
* **Estimated cost (in NIS)**: 0 - 5,000 (depending on the current state of the premises and required improvements)
* **Estimated time to complete**: Ongoing
* **Responsible authority**: Ministry of Health
* **Priority level**: High
* **Useful links**: 
	+ https://www.gov.il/en/departments/minstry-of-health
	+ https://www.gov.il/BlobFolder/generalpage/uniform-spec-04-02a/he/18-07-2022_4.2A.pdf (Section 2.1)
